About me

Hello, I'm Anastasija Scepak. A web developer/designer, currently located in United Kingdom. I'm a passionate web developer/designer who likes to think out of the box and use my imagination while drafting, sketching and developing/designing beautiful, yet interesting websites.

I've graduated as a bachelor in philology from Vilnius University (Kaunas Faculty of Humanities) in 2013. I always was interested in web design/development, but was afraid to start it. To become a web developer/designer I was encouraged by my family, boyfriend and friends before graduating. But at that moment my mind was set at becoming a translator.

After doing freelance translations I understood that don't want to do it anymore and decided to go for web designer/developer job. My very first experience of making a website was when I was 13 years old. I didn't know what I was doing and there was no decent design, still that experience helped me in studying web design and web development on online courses.
If you'd like to follow me on Twitter, my user name is @na7tiu6ka.
