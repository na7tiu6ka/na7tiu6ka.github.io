About me

I'm Anastasija Scepak. A front end web developer/designer, currently located in United Kingdom. I'm a passionate front end web developer/designer who likes to think out of the box and use my imagination while drafting, sketching and developing/designing beautiful, yet interesting websites.

I've graduated as a bachelor in philology from Vilnius University (Kaunas Faculty of Humanities) in 2013. I always was interested in web design/development, but was afraid to start it. In becoming a front end web developer/designer I was encouraged by my partner before graduating. But at that moment my mind was set at becoming a translator.

After doing freelance translations I understood that don't want to do it anymore and decided to go for front end web design/development. My very first experience of making a website was when I was 13 years old. I didn't know what I was doing and there was no decent design, still that experience helped me in studying online courses in front end web design/development.
If you'd like to follow me on Twitter, my user name is @na7tiu6ka.
