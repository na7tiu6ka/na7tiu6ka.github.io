Who am I?

I'm Stacie, a web designer originally from Lithuania and now based in the UK. My web design journey began in 2003 when I discovered a Harry Potter-themed website dedicated to Hermione Granger. Inspired by that, I created my own Harry Potter website.

Though I took a different path in school, in 2013, my boyfriend (now husband) encouraged me to dive back into web design. Since then, I've been loving every moment and challenging myself to learn new things. My skills include HTML, CSS, Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, XD, and some After Effects.

You can find illustrations and some animations on my Instagram. Besides web design, I enjoy photography, vector illustration, reading, playing the violin, and exploring new places. Follow me on Twitter @StacieStreams, and get in touch via LinkedIn. You can also catch me on Twitch, sharpening my skills.
