Who am I?

I'm Anastasija, but usually I introduce myself as Stacie. Originally I come from Lithuania, where I've graduated from Vilnius University as a translator. I've always had a keen interest (since I was 13 years old, to be precise) in web development & design, but was too afraid to learn it. The encouragement was from my fiance and at the end of 2013 I have started my journey as a Web Designer/Developer.

I'm a passionate Web Designer/Developer who enjoys building interesting and high quality websites.
I like to think outside of the box and use imagination while drafting, sketching and designing beautiful yet simple websites.
Besides being interested in web design, I enjoy photography, vector illustration, reading books, playing violin, travelling and exploring new places.
