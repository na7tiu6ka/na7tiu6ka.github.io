Names & Faces app design

Oh boy, let me tell you about this awesome task that came up in our weekly challenge lineup. It was called Names & Faces, and it just happened to be the very first challenge I tackled. The mission? Designing an app tailor-made for teachers or university lecturers, helping them remember their students with utmost ease.

Now, I wanted to inject a touch of brilliance into this app, so my main idea was to keep things simple and straightforward. But hey, who says simplicity can't be fun? I aimed to create an experience that would bring a smile to the faces of educators, making their interactions with the app an absolute joyride.
